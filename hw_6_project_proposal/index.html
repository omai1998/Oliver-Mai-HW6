RoastMe AI

Turn any TikTok trend into a customized celebrity roast with AI deepfakes.

Motivation: The rise of reaction videos and celebrity roasts on platforms like TikTok and Instagram Reels shows a massive appetite for comedic, personalized content. Getting a famous personality like Dana White to react to a specific niche video is essentially impossible for the average user. We want to bridge this gap by allowing anyone to be "roasted" by their favorite celebrity, capitalizing on the viral nature of reaction content and giving users a tool to create highly engaging, humorous videos.

Business/User Value: Content creators and everyday users benefit by gaining access to highly engaging, viral-ready videos. A customized celebrity deepfake reacting to niche trends offers a high "shareability" factor, driving organic growth and user retention.

Components to Build: We will construct a multi-step pipeline: a frontend UI for video upload and persona selection; a Vision-Language Model to analyze the uploaded TikTok video; a customized LLM to generate the 10-15 second roast script/storyboard in the celebrity's voice; an API integration with Kling to generate the deepfake video; and a video processing module to stitch the deepfake to the original video.
